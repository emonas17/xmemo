### Subtitle

### Promotional Text

### Short Description
Xmemo is a simple voice reminder app for moments when typing is too slow. Record a quick memo, choose today, tomorrow, a custom date, or repeating weekdays, then hear the original recording when the reminder appears.

### Full Description
Xmemo helps you capture reminders with your voice and hear them later.

Instead of typing a note, tap record, say what you need to remember, and choose when the reminder should appear. When the time comes, Xmemo opens a clean playback screen where you can listen, snooze for 15 minutes or 1 hour, move it to tomorrow, or stop repeating reminders.

Features:
- Quick voice recording for reminders
- Playback screen built for one-tap listening
- Today, tomorrow, custom date and time options
- Repeating reminders by weekday
- Reminder sound choices
- Optional playback through speaker or earpiece
- Automatic short reminder title from the beginning of the recording
- Lithuanian and English interface support

Xmemo is designed for speed: speak now, remember later.
